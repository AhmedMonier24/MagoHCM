attendance Module
With Mago you can manage your employees hours, shifts and late arrivals by linking to the fingerprint machine in the company. And helps you calculate total worked hours including overtime, vacations, sick leaves and holidays.
Payroll Module
We know the pain/suffering to manage their payroll, salaries are calculated by an automated way after deductions and taxes are deducted whether the salaries are cash or bank transfer. The system can adapt with any tax systems and rules.
organization Module
The Mago system is flexible and smooth to show you what your company looks like easily. An organization chart graphically represents an organization's structure, explanation of the whole hierarchy (jobs, department), to define the tasks for each employee, to determine the positions and who is headed by whom
Self Services Module
One of the most time and effort saving modules, Employees are allowed to enter the system and apply for any request they want. They can do it from mobile phones or the web to submit request for leave or permission, employees can show own payslips.
performance Module
The target of any company is to achieve their goals with strong team, your team needs to know what’s expected of them to achieve their targets, Performance management maintains periodical evaluations of the organization employees’ performance.
Also, you can manage employee’s performance in a deeply and measurable way with OKR:
OKR: Objectives Key Results is used to achieve the desired goal by shared the organization with the intention of providing teams with visibility of goals with the intention to align and focus effort.
Reqruitment Module

With this module collecting important candidates depended on existing requirements is effortless. From here you can invite them to an interview.
traininig Module
Enhance your team is a must in your company, you can build the skillsets of existing employees, one way to do that is to provide your employees this opportunity to help you achieve your goals. You have the ability to create a (KPI’s) to evaluate the employee performance.
